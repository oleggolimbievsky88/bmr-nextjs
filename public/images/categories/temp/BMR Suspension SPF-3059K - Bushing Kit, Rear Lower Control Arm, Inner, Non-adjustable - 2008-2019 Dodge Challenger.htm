Description

Reduce deflection, improve handling, and maintain a comfortable quiet ride with elastomer bushing from SuperPro. SuperPro bushings are made from low-deflection elastomer. Elastomer is a viscoelastic polymer that has many properties that make it far superior to both OEM rubber bushings and polyurethane bushings. It is known for its high stiffness, low friction, and excellent stability, which greatly reduces bushing deflection that occurs during acceleration, cornering, and braking. Each SuperPro bushing is designed to exact tolerances, allowing for a perfect fit and increased performance for any driving style. The SuperPro elastomer bushings greatly increase the rigidity and stability of the chassis, suspension, or sway bars to provide maximum control and consistency, with all of the articulation necessary for any form of high-performance driving.

SuperPro elastomer bushings are perfect for any vehicle used for performance driving. Replacing the bushing in the rear suspension decreases deflection and increases suspension stability over stock rubber bushings. OE cradle and control arm bushings are designed from soft rubber and produce excessive suspension movement during normal and aggressive driving. SuperPro elastomer bushings minimizing deflection, giving you better response, improved overall feel, and increasing suspension performance. The elastomer material is compliant enough to absorb NVH and allow for enough articulation for great handling. This incredible combination gives you a much-improved overall feel in any performance-driving situation.

SuperPro bushing kits are available in blue with zinc-coated hardware. Installation time may vary depending on stock bushing removal technique.

Kit contains four rear lower control arm bushings (inner) and zinc coated sleeves. Sold as a kit.

All SuperPro bushings come with a “Lifetime Warranty, with no Exclusions”. For more information, visit the www.superpro.com.au/warranty or contact BMR Suspension for details.
